About Us

STEEL & CRETE Structural Solutions is an LLP located in Ernakulam, offering high end engineering solutions. In keeping with the world’s recent industrial developments, Steel & Crete offer consulting services to all types of civil & structural engineering applications. The team members have worked with many of the renowned architects and clients always striving to find better methods to tackle the engineering challenges. Our Engineers were involved in prestigious projects in India and abroad, expertise in the field of structural design, retrofitting, building construction and vibration problems and have also executed a wide range of projects like nuclear power plants, high rise buildings, pre-engineered structures, bridges and machine foundations.

Leadership

Founder 1 - CEO

Well-experienced in implementing innovative and economical designs for structural systems in collaboration with other stakeholders like architects, contractors, geotechnical experts, MEP, and fire consultants. With nearly 25 years of experience in structural design and retrofitting, he has worked on a diverse set of RCC, PSC, Steel buildings, tanks, towers, machine foundations, and industrial & power plant structures. His expertise includes wind tunnel and shake table tests, aseismic design of nuclear power plants, multiscale modelling, vibration studies on bridges due to high-speed express trains, and sustainable structures. Presently, he is focusing more on the design of composite and/or sustainable structures/architectural components made up from a wide spectrum of materials.
